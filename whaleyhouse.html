Activities at the Whaley House

If your looking for a good scare while in the midst of San Diego, this is the Destination for you! We offer basic visits to experience the presence of the Whaley family. Scary folktales are told which lure the spirits of the human beings that once lived in the very rooms into your presence. Last but certainly not least, after hour private tours are our most popular attraction. Come and stay a while and get to know the spirits of the Whaley family, but dont get too comfortable in their space!
